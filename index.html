Process

How I usually approach a service or delivery challenge.

01

Identify the problem clearly

I define the operational issue first, whether it is inefficiency, poor visibility, or a broken workflow affecting delivery.

02

Analyse workflows and data

I review how things currently work, where handoffs break down, and what the available data says about the problem.

03

Design a practical solution

I turn the problem into a workable improvement, whether that is an automation, dashboard, reporting structure, or service redesign.

04

Build and test quickly

I use available tools like Excel, AI tools, and lightweight automation to test ideas without overcomplicating delivery.

05

Measure impact

I track results through clear metrics such as time saved, attendance, target delivery, revenue impact, or improved visibility.

06

Iterate for scale

I refine based on results and focus on solutions that are practical, scalable, and easier for teams to maintain.
